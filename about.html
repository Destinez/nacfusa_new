Nifes Associate Community Fellowship, North America

NACF is the National Body for all NIFES Graduates in North America, consisting of U.S and Canada. NACF North America was inaugurated in 2012 with the influence of Bro Ebere Eze, the then NIFES Director of Associates, who visited some brethren in New Jersey that year.

Eligible Members of the NIFES Associate Community Fellowship, North America
  • All Graduates of higher institu tions of Nigerian Schools who attended NIFES.
  • All organized local campus Alumni groups in the United States and Canada
  • Interested believers from other Nigerian campus fellowships residing in the United States and Canada
